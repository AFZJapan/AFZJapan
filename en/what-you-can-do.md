      <h2 class="section-title">Examples of what Apartheid Free Zones can do</h2>

<div class="page">
Each Apartheid Free Zone are not just about individual consumer choice, but individuals can create their own way to dedicate themselves to the BDS campaign and to the Palestinian people. It seeks to cultivate spaces of genuine, profound ties with many others. These are only a part of many things that we can do.
<br>

<p>
<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">1. Endorsement</span></h6>
The crucial goal of the BDS campaign and Apartheid Free Zones is to change the social attitude that normalizes the Human Rights violations and the occupation and colonization of Israel. Israel dissuades the people from justifying their apartheid policy and occupation through different enterprises, institutions, and businesses in the world. Therefore, to expose the fact that Israel’s purpose of occupation and the manipulation of reality are very important, as part of the boycott, we would request you to post up our “Basis of Unity” in the spaces when you become an AFZ space. Also, publicizing your statement and sharing information about Palestine will expand the circle of solidarity with Palestine in your space.

<ul>
  <li><a href="https://afzjapan.com/en/register">Register</a> your organization and add your space in AFZ</li>
  <li>Make your statement and sign on petitions such as <a href="https://www.cultureagainstapartheid.jp/" target="_blank" data-cke-saved-href="https://www.cultureagainstapartheid.jp/">Culture Against Apartheid</a></li>
  <li> Use AFZ signs, stickers and brochures to show publicly your spaces, and to attach AFZ links in your websites.</li> 
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">2. Boycotting products and companies are complicit</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li><span class="bold">Review of suppliers</span>: Do not handle the products that were produced in the occupied lands and settlement.</li>
  <li><span class="bold">Use the boycott list</span>: Refer to official BDS list <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/DTCbx6VAbnL/?img_index=1">(BDSJapan's posts)</a> and reliable sources to identify products to boycott.</li>
  <li><span class="bold">Find alternative suppliers</span>: Proactively choose Palestinian products and fair-trade products.</li>
</ul>

<a href="https://bdsjapanbulletin.wordpress.com/2024/01/27/%e3%81%a4%e3%81%aa%e3%81%8c%e3%82%8b%e6%8a%80%e8%a1%93/" target="_blank">
  <img src="/assets/img/BDSBoycotts.jpeg" style="border-radius: 10px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; margin-bottom: 20px; width: 50%;" />
</a>
</p>

<p>
<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">3. Protest against all forms of oppression and inequality</span></h6>
<p>BDS opposes all forms of oppression and inequality, which is based on intersectionality. The issues in Palestine are caused by racism and oppression; therefore, we respect the human rights of all and oppose all forms of racism and oppression.</p>

<p>AFZ pursues that our spaces are free from racism, sexual violence, and violence of colonialism, class system, and free from inequality in gender, religion, and on the base of capability.</p>
</p>

<button type="button" class="collapsible">What Schools and Universities Can Do</button>
<div class="content">
  <p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Examples that educational institutions and laboratories can do：</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Add endorsement of AFZ in your syllabus</li>
  <li>Use the AFZ logo and introduce AFZ when you give a talk or lectures</li>
  <li>Add endorsement of AFZ in the publication of your academic associations, and make an announcement at the beginning of conferences and meetings.</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Educate about the boycott in academics</span></h6>
<p>As the guideline of the Palestinian Campaign for Academic and Cultural Boycott of Israel, oppose any form of justification of Israel’s oppression over Palestinians.</p>
<ul>
  <li>The educational and cultural institutions take a crucial role in the strategy to justify and to continue the crimes of the settler-colonialism, apartheid, and the military of Israel. PACBI therefore gives a boycott guideline to cut off Israeli cultural and academic society, especially universities, particularly in the field of military research and racism study, with any forms of relationships, such as events, conferences or in the form of accords.<br />PACBI Guidelines：<br/><a href="https://bdsmovement.net/academic-boycott" target="_blank" data-cke-saved-href="https://bdsmovement.net/academic-boycott">https://bdsmovement.net/academic-boycott</a></li>
</ul>
<!-- p>京都大学X研究室の例：</p -->

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored" style="line-height: 20pt;">Check your university's relationships with universities in Israel and military research firms</span></h6>
<p>Check whether your educational institutions have a relationship with Israeli universities and the firms that are complicit in the genocide of Palestinians and support the settler-colonialism of Israel, or the government of Israel. Demand that your institutions cut off the relationship with Israel if they are in one.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Dedicated to sustainability in Human Rights</span></h6>
<p>Learn about racism through the history of Palestine and the situation there right now. Learn about colonialism in the history of Japan and the world, which is unshakably laid as a base of racism that we have now and affects our everyday life.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored" style="line-height: 20pt;">Develop networks that oppose apartheid, and strengthen them by connecting with other AFZ</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Plan projects and activities together with other AFZ affiliated universities and educational institutions</li>
  <li>Make study projects about AFZ and BDS with other affiliates to expand the network.</li>
  <li>Check in BNC website if you are AFZ overseas<br/><a href="https://bdsmovement.net/apartheid-free-zones" target="_blank" data-cke-saved-href="https://bdsmovement.net/apartheid-free-zones">https://bdsmovement.net/apartheid-free-zones</a></li>
</ul>

</p>

<button type="button" class="collapsible">What Religious Institutions Can Do</button>
<div class="content">
  <p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Clarify the solidarity with Palestine</span></h6>
<p>Israel tries to justify these abominable massacres and the oppression as the cause of their different religion from Palestinians, and uses historical Christian animosity against Islam to support their crimes. However, there are various religions practiced in Palestine, and there is nothing that can justify genocide. 
</p>
<p>Many religious authorities in the world, including Jewish rabbis, are rejecting the use of religion to justify the genocide. Therefore, because Israel uses religion for justify their atrocious genocide, rejecting their justification by religious people and religious organizations with religious causes is quite significant, and it is crucial responsibility. Moreover, showing sympathy for and solidarity with Palestinians, overcoming the differences in their religions by the authorities, can be admirable.</p>
<p>Publicize your space as an Apartheid Free Zone and show your solidarity with Palestine,  and have media releases.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Release your own statement or sign an existing one, such as the one at <a href="https://www.cultureagainstapartheid.jp/" data-cke-saved-href="https://www.cultureagainstapartheid.jp/">https://www.cultureagainstapartheid.jp/</a>.</li>
  <li>Talk about the solidarity with Palestine and show remorse for what has been lost in Palestine in sermons</li>
  <li>Use stickers and our website links to show that your space is AFZ</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored" style="line-height: 20pt;">Talk about the genocide in Palestine</span></h6>
<p>Israel’s crimes against humanity, including genocide began from the establishment of Israel, and it has continued to today. However, the world, including Japan, has ignored it, and ignoring this crime against humanity destroys our ethics and international morale.  It therefore needs a sustainable system of education that brings us to understand racism and encourages us to keep rejecting racism continuously.</p>
<p>Learn about racism through the history of Palestine and the situation there right now. Learn about colonialism in the history of Japan and the world, which is solidly laid as a base of racism that we have now and affects our everyday life.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Talk about Palestine in Sunday service</li>
  <li>Talk about Palestine and the area within Bible study</li>
  <li>write articles in your publications</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Take a role in the community</span></h6>
<p>There are many people who individually worry about Palestinians, but the issue is overwhelming as a person. Develop circles and give talks about Palestine, and it would encourage people to get more involved when someone presents supportive ideas or expresses solidarity.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Remorse</span></h6>
<p>Remorse for the deceased in Palestine is crucial as supporting those who live. Let’s express remorse for the deceased at prayer.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Hold support events and gather donations</span></h6>
<p>In Gaza, there is continuing starvation caused by Israel’s blocking trucks from entering with supplies. People who live with genocide need support with International aid supplies. Make plans for solidarity events to support their needs. You can collect donations that support them directly.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Reject Zionism</span></h6>
<p>Sometime Israelis said that Zionism is a religion; however, Theodor Herzl, the one who proposed it, was an atheist, and Zionism is rooted in colonialism. There are some religious organizations that endorsed Zionism in Japan, but we should cut them out and create an atmosphere that rejects Zionism.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored" style="line-height: 20pt;">Develop networks that oppose apartheid, and strengthen them by connecting with other AFZ</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Plan projects and activities together with other AFZ affiliate churches, temples, mosques, synagogues</li>
  <li>Make study projects about AFZ and BDS with other affiliates to expand the network</li>
  <li>Check in BNC website if you are AFZ overseas.<br/><a href="https://bdsmovement.net/apartheid-free-zones" data-cke-saved-href="https://bdsmovement.net/apartheid-free-zones">https://bdsmovement.net/apartheid-free-zones</a></li>
</ul>

</p>

<button type="button" class="collapsible">What Community Councils and the Government of States Can Do</button>
<div class="content">
  <p>

<p>Community councils and local governments can endorse the basis of unity of the BDS movement and strategies along with our guidelines, and they can also play a role in extending solidarity with Palestine. Refrain from purchasing any products and services from Israel and from companies complicit with Israel supporting human rights violations of Palestinians and the colonial occupation of Palestinian lands. Work independently in support of boycott, divestment and sanctions activities against Israel.</p>
<p>At first, learn about the BDS movement, which was started by Palestinian’s call to the world, and think about what governmental administration can do.</p>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Set Out the Norm of Ethics</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Set out the international law as a base of the administration ethics, and strengthen their system to cut off companies that are complicit with occupation and war crimes that are against International Law.</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Approve sanctions</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Approve resolution about Israel ending its occupation and colonization of all Palestinian lands and dismantling the wall</li>
  <li>Approve the sanctions about boycott and divestment from Israel.</li>

  <li> Approve the resolution about ending the Israel’s setter-colonialism and Palestinian racial cleansing as genocide.</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Review the bidding and contracts with the companies you have business with</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li><span class="bold">Check the companies that are complicit with Israel’s occupation and the genocide in the business of bidding and contracting</span></li>
  <ul>
    <li>BDS boycott list includes Axa, HP, Sumitomo corporation.</li>
    <li>Boycott the companies having business with illegal setters in Palestinian lands. ( Banks, construction companies and security guard companies)</li>
    <li>Do not attend any projects and events that the Israel government sponsored.</li>
    <li>The list of companies to boycott in Japan. <a href="https://bdsjapanbulletin.wordpress.com/2024/01/27/%E3%81%A4%E3%81%AA%E3%81%8C%E3%82%8B%E6%8A%80%E8%A1%93/" target="_blank" data-cke-saved-href="https://bdsjapanbulletin.wordpress.com/2024/01/27/%E3%81%A4%E3%81%AA%E3%81%8C%E3%82%8B%E6%8A%80%E8%A1%93/">https://bdsjapanbulletin.wordpress.com/2024/01/27/つながる技術/</a></li>
  </ul>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Divest public funds and the Pension Fund</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Check whether public funds and Pension funds that local government handles are invested in the companies complicit with Israel occupation policy.</li>
  <li>Establish an ethical guideline for divestment from military firms and human rights violators.</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Check Sister-Cities and International Cooperation</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li>Reconsider relations with Israeli municipalities: Review sister city agreements with cities in Israel.</li>
  <li>Establish good relationships with cities in Gaza and The West Bank, and develop good cooperative relationships and support.</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="afz-heading-small-colored">Educate about Human Rights and Citizenship</span></h6>
<ul>
  <li><span class="bold">Pursue ending all forms of discriminations and oppression</span></li>
  <li><span class="bold">Teach people to understand about the BDS movement</span>: Through lecture series, exhibitions, classes, and events, people can learn human rights in Palestine.</li>
  <li><span class="bold">Introduce culture of Palestine</span>: Through Films, art exhibitions, literature festivals,  Learn history of Palestine and cultures.</li>
</ul>
